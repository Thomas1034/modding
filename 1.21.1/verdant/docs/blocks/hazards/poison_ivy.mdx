# Poison Ivy

**Poison Ivy** is a common sight in the **Verdant Growth**, often seen hanging from trees or cave ceilings. Entities that walk through **Poison Ivy** - or stand on **Poison Ivy Covered Strangler Leaves** - will be inflicted with the **Poison** effect for a short duration.
